A high volume of patients in local clinic

Issue 2

The ineffecincy of manual patient data management by doctors

Rural Healthcare Access

Rural area with limited access to healthcare resources

Arduino

Used to bridge the sensors and the system and make them more stable

AS608 fingerprint sensor

Used to identify patients and ensure data privacy

Cable

Used to connect the sensors and the Arduino to ensure stable data transmission

Case

Used to house the components and make the device portable and easy to use

Emergency Response

Rapid emergency response system that alerts medical teams and provides critical patient information during emergencies.

Data Analytics

Advanced data analytics to identify trends, predict health outcomes, and improve overall healthcare delivery.

User Interface Design

Intuitive and user-friendly interface designed for ease of use by medical professionals with varying technical expertise.

API Integration

Robust API integration allowing seamless connection with existing hospital systems and third-party healthcare applications.

Compliance and Privacy

Full compliance with healthcare regulations and privacy standards to protect patient data and maintain legal requirements.

Scalability

Scalable architecture designed to handle growing patient databases and expanding healthcare facility requirements.
